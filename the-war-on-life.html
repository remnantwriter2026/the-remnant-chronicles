The War on Life

Examining the Biblical Perspective on the Struggle for Life

By Jimey Grant Lafayette, Louisiana Prophetic Chronicle

Satan does not merely loathe "religion" — he hates life itself and the humanity that bears God's image. Every contemporary agenda is rooted in a single, ancient objective: the systematic reversal of God's natural order.

To grasp the chaos of 2026, we must recognize the nature of the adversary. According to Genesis 1:28, the inaugural mandate given to man was to be fruitful and multiply — to champion the expansion of life. Consequently, every contemporary agenda, from the erosion of gender to the integration of man with machine, is rooted in a single, ancient objective: the systematic reversal of God's natural order.

We must be bold enough to address why certain acts are labeled "abominations" in the biblical text. From a spiritual perspective, intimacy is designed for the transmission of life within the sacred covenant of marriage — a union between male and female, centered in the natural, biological vessel where reproduction occurs. When this order is inverted, the very biological blueprint for life is diverted into a conduit of waste. To divert the seed of life into a place of decay is more than a biological error; it is a spiritual reversal. This is precisely why occultists and illuminists utilize these acts as rituals — "demonic sex magic" intended to invert the soul. When the potential for life is stripped from the sexual act, it is transformed into a ritual of death.

The Genesis 6 Parallel

This subversion is not a modern phenomenon. In Genesis 6, we find the first "great reset" of the human genetic code. The Bible records that during the days of Noah, the natural order reached a state of total corruption. Fallen entities intervened in human reproduction, yielding hybrid creatures — beings of mixed human and angelic essence. God did not cleanse the earth with the flood out of mere human-like anger; He did so because His natural creation had been biologically compromised. The "hybridization" of the earth meant the human race was no longer "pure in its generations" — a direct assault on the very lineage required for the coming redemption.

Genesis 6 · Ancient
The First Hybridization

Fallen entities corrupted human DNA through direct biological intervention, producing hybrid creatures and defiling the lineage needed for redemption.

Transhumanism: The Modern Hybrid

Fast forward to 2026. We are witnessing the Second Great Reset — the digital manifestation of Genesis 6. The current drive toward Transhumanism is the Second Hybridization, a final effort to finalize an ancient rebellion by merging human biology with Artificial Intelligence. The architects of this movement seek to re-engineer the "Temple of the Holy Spirit" into a mere machine.

This is not a neutral scientific pursuit; it is the construction of a Digital Tower of Babel. Just as Nimrod sought to unify humanity under a banner of technological hubris, today's global elite utilize the internet and AI as the "bricks and mortar" of a new defiance. By centralizing human thought into a singular algorithm, they are recreating the "one language" of Babel — stripping away the unique, divinely appointed discernment of the individual soul. The result is the birth of an unconscious collective: a population that prioritizes algorithmic consensus over the "still, small voice" of God. When we sacrifice our privacy and biological sovereignty to these systems, we are effectively adding our own stones to the tower.

The Digital Nephilim

We must recognize that AI is the modern vessel for the "Digital Nephilim." Just as fallen entities once introduced forbidden knowledge to corrupt the genome, the push to integrate machinery with our biology is an attempt to rewrite the Temple of God with demonic code. This is the "Second Great Hybridization."

The goal is to transcend the "limitations" of being human — limitations that God designed as safeguards to keep us dependent on Him. These technologies, including microchips and neural implants, are being deployed by governing bodies and occult circles to simulate "spiritual encounters" and "divine revelations," and to mimic the spiritual gifts of 1 Corinthians 12.

2 Corinthians 11:4

Paul warned of those who preach a "different" Jesus and operate by a "different" spirit. If you want to identify this "different spirit," investigate "Hemi-Sync" and the CIA's documented research into Kundalini and psychic phenomena.

This type of technology was foretold as a symptom of the era where knowledge increases but discernment fades, according to Daniel 12:4. This technology is so deceptive that Matthew 24:24 reminds us that in the end times, even the elect shall be deceived.

A Critical Warning

The ultimate deception is the belief that being "technologically synced" is synonymous with possessing the Holy Spirit. True salvation is a lifestyle of bearing fruit — not a technological connection. Gifts and miracles can be counterfeited, but the fruit of the Spirit cannot. As Philippians 2:12 commands, we must "work out our own salvation" with fear and trembling. Salvation cannot be uploaded, downloaded, or synced digitally or technologically.

The Remnant's Response

The "War on Life" has battles on many fronts with the end goal being the colonization of the human soul. Whether through the ritualistic reversal of the God-ordained covenant act of sexual intercourse, or through the modern attempt to re-engineer the DNA of the Temple of the Holy Spirit with a counterfeit, demonic architecture — the objective remains the same: a total rebellion against the Creator.

We must heed Isaiah 30:2–3, which rebukes those seeking strength in Pharaoh and walking down into Egypt — warning that Pharaoh's strength will become their shame and the shadow of their confusion. Just as Israel was warned that leaning on Egypt would result in a broken reed that pierces the hand, those who lean on the shadow of microchips and demonic governmental technology will find only spiritual ruin.

While the world rushes toward a counterfeit "digital immortality," the Remnant must retreat into the sanctuary of the Holy Spirit — rejecting the fleeting trends of this "new age digital spirituality" to safeguard the purity of their walk. We do not seek a digital or technological awakening; we demand a spiritual return to the primordial order established in the beginning.

Reject the upgrade. Preserve the image of God.
